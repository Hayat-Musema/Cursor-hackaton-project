Ethiopia's Tech Scene

Explore the vibrant technology ecosystem in Ethiopia, from innovative startups to established tech companies and thriving developer communities.

Leading Tech Companies in Ethiopia

Ethio Telecom

Ethiopia's largest telecommunications company, providing internet, mobile, and fixed-line services across the country.

  • Mobile and internet services
  • Digital transformation initiatives
  • 5G network development

Commercial Bank of Ethiopia

Leading financial institution with significant investments in digital banking and fintech solutions.

  • Digital banking platforms
  • Mobile money services
  • E-commerce integration

Jumia Ethiopia

E-commerce platform bringing online shopping and digital commerce to Ethiopian consumers.

  • Online marketplace
  • Digital payments
  • Logistics solutions

  • Food delivery services

EdTech Ethiopia

AgriTech Solutions

  • Startup incubation

  • Market access support
